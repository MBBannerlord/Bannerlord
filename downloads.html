Installing Main Game:
  1. Download Mount & Blade Bannerlord, thanks to Gold-Team for uploading.
  2. Download Epic Games Launcher.
  3. Launch Epic Games, search for Hello Neighbor Mod Kit and click on Get (don't download it).
  4. Run the game through Launcher.exe, which is located in the game folder, once you do that, you can launch the game through Epic Games by clicking on Hello Mod Kit.
Installing PE & GOT Modules:
  1. Download Persistent Empires mode for Bannerlord.
  2. Download Game Of Thrones mode for Bannerlord.
  3. Extract both modes in Mount And Blade II Bannerlord\Modules

  4. Launch the game.
  5. Click on multiplayer and then mods, now you need to modify your modes as same as this picture.

  6. Click on PLAY and enjoy.
